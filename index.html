Wanna get to know more about me?


Hi there! I'm Cherry Mea, an animation and multimedia arts student who's passionate about blending technology, art, and storytelling. Born and raised in Ozamity City, Misamis Occidental. As I continue to learn and grow in the world of animation and multimedia, I’m excited to share my journey, showcase my work, and connect with others who share a love for creative expression. Whether you're here to learn more about my work, creative projects, hobbies, etc. or just to get a glimpse into my journey, I’m glad you stopped by. Through this site, I aim to connect with like-minded individuals, showcase the work I’m proud of, and share insights that I hope you'll find valuable. Here, you'll find my works or projects I've done. It’s a little slice of my life, and I’m excited to invite you in.

Feel free to browse around, explore my latest work, and get in touch if you’d like to collaborate or chat about anything creative.
